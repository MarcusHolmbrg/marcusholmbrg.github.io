About me


I am a recently graduated student from the Media technology programme at KTH (Royal Institute of Technology) in Stockholm Sweden. My track focused on visiual media and throughout my education I have gotten to work with everything from UX/UI, machine learning and web design.

My main area of interest however, relates to the Unity game engine. First getting into Unity back in 2018, I've since used it for all sorts of projects and experiments. Some of these projects have been part of my studies while others have come about on my free time and stemmed from curiosity and me playing around with Unity as a hobby.
